LESSONS LEARNED

RESEARCH
On this particular project thorough research on the new platform had not been carried out. This led to features being added and removed throughout the process, as some components that were assumed to be out of the box actually involved extra cost and developemnt time.
PLANNING
Thorough planning is paramount in an efficient project build. There were various design overhauls of the site, as there was no strong UI UX design outline in place prior to the site build.
SEPARATION OF CONCERNS
Collaboration is extremly important, and everyone should have a voice. But at a certain point subject matter experts have to be given responsibility and full control over their own aread of project input.
IMPORTANCE OF SUPPORT
Good documentation and a helpful point of contact within the platform provider are essential in the development process when dealing with blockers
TEAMWORK
A project of this size really emphasised how vital it is to have a strong and cohesive in place. Everyone can and should have the opportunity to contribute, and not always within their own area of expertise.
